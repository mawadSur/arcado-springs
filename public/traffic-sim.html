This is an illustrative simulation, not a traffic study. The animation is a simplified model for communication; vehicle counts and trip lengths are schematic. The relationships it shows are grounded in published planning research: walkable mixed-use sites internalize roughly 15–30% of trips (higher at the evening peak), and compact development cuts vehicle-miles-traveled by about 20–40% versus dispersed, single-use sprawl (ITE/NCHRP 684, the National Household Travel Survey, EPA Smart Growth, Ewing & Cervero 2010).
